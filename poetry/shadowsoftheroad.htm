.:Shadows of the Road:.

.2002.

Every shadow in the road becomes a sea
Of virtual hatred and dark agony
Every breeze in the forest is like a song
That sets my heart to yearn and long
Every sigh of a child is like a scream
To show me that the nothing is what it seems
Every blink of the eyes is an eternity
Of a life with no solidarity
Every change of the tide becomes a new day
For a darkness where dead men lay
And each and every breath I take becomes the rain
To wash away the darkness of life and of pain
