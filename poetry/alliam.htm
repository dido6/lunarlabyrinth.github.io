.:All I Am:.

.2002.

I watch the rain rolling across the shores
Saturating the sand where waves cannot roll
I watch the snow fall silent on the mountain
But even the great snows cannot the mountain control

It is like a woman breathing hard during love
A feeling no man can touch but has caused with a glance
It is that woman waking alone
Knowing there would never be another chance

It is me standing in a crowded room
But feeling absolutely alone
It is me kneeling before the heavens
And screaming out all that I atone

All I am is everything no one understands
All I am is waiting to be free, lying stark
So that all I am anyone can see
But all I am is alone, dying in the dark
