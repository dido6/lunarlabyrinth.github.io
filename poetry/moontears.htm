.:Moon's Tears:.

.2000.

As moonlight fades to darkness
An evil shadow appears
Taking and destroying everything
Even the dragon’s tears

The moon does not die easy
But its strength and light go out
As crystal tears begin to fall
Only darkness is about

Alone in the dark
No knight to guide the way
The moon follows a path
Where evil is at play

Black wings over take her
And the lights all but fade
Into a diamond prison
That evil dreams have made

Only the love of an Emerald
And a shard burning bright
Can save the precious moon
From the darkened night
