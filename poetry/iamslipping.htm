.:I am Slipping:.

.2002.

Slipping behing a crowd of faces
Happy faces
Behind a shroud of darkness
I am curled
Behind the crimson tapestry
Pushed away to the cold stone
The stones of my own castle tower
I am curled hiding
Forgotten
Haunted
Behind a glimmer
Behind a sigh
And behind my own soft smile
