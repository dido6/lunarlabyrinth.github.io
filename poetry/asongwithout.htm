.:A Song Without Life and a Poem in Death:.

.2002.

How can you have a song without a song
If so, it can’t be a song very long
Without the song, music would be gone
And then where would we be

How can you have a poem without a poem
What to do with an empty book or tome
Blank pages with no words to roam
Where would that leave me?

How can you have a life without a life
Passing days in meaningless strife
A shattered soul no longer living life
That is where I’ll be

How can you have a death without death
A soul that still takes a breath
Alive but living in death
If I am alive then that death has claimed me
