.:Ghosts of Loneliness:.

.2002.

The ghosts of yesterday
Haunt my dreams
My dreams so full of promise
My dreams so full of lies
They haunt with shadows on the wall
And soft voices in the wind
In my dreams so full of life
My dreams so full of their deaths
Whisperings of love and laughter
Sobbing of life gone wrong
In my dreams so covered in rapture
In my dreams where I am alone
Alone with the voices of the past
Alone with the lives of those that never lived
But I live, I live in my dreams
In my dreams where still all is lost
And in my dreams the dead whisper
The living, love, but fade
And I am always left alone
